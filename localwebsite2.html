Advantages of forest

Forests offer a wide array of advantages, impacting the environment, human well-being, and the economy. They act as vital carbon sinks, regulate water cycles, and support biodiversity. Additionally, forests provide resources like timber, food, and medicinal plants, while also offering recreational and aesthetic benefits.
